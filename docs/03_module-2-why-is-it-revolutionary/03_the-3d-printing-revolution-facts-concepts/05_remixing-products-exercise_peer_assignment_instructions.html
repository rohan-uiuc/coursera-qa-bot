The goal of this second exercise is to give you some experience remixing objects. In essence, a digital iPhone case is really not much different than a digital iTunes song. So, now you can remix objects just as easily as you can remix music. My hope is by engaging in this remixing activity, you will gain a firsthand understanding of the revolutionary potential of 3D printing.

To engage in this remixing exercise, we will be using Thingiverse . As we discussed earlier, Thingiverse is essentially an iTunes store for objects. However, in addition to downloading designs made by others, you can also remix many of these designs using its Customizer feature.

  1. Open Thingiverse and create an account if you do not already have one.

  2. Find a Customizable design that you would like to remix (you can easily find customizable designs under the "Create" menu bar.

  3. Click on this object.

  4. Once it is open, click on the "Open in Customizer" to the right of the image.

  5. The design will show up with some menu which will allow you to change parameters for the design. This is 'Remixing'

  6. Once you are done, hit the blue "Create Thing" button on the top right of the image.

  7. Save and publish your remixed design on Thingiverse. (Note: It may take up to 24 hours before your Thing is published).

  8. On the My submission tab, record the Thing number for your remixed design, state why you picked this design to remix, and briefly discuss your thoughts and feeling about this remixing process.

You may save a draft of your work as you go, and you can come back later to continue working on your draft. When you are finished working, click the Preview button, verify your identity, and then click Submit for review to submit the assignment.

Review criteria

You are required to evaluate the submissions of at least THREE of your peers based on the instructions and rubric provided. You may begin giving feedback to other students as soon as you submit your assignment; click the Review classmates tab to begin. Feel free to provide additional reviews beyond the three required!

Every submission will need to include the following.

  1. The thing number for your remixed design

  2. Why you picked this design to be remixed

  3. Your thoughts about the remixing process

Each part of the submission will be graded on the following scale:

The Thing number for your remixed design

  • 0 points: Does not provide the required information

  • 1 point: Provides the required information

Why you picked this design to be remixed

  • 0 points: Does not provide the required information or does not answer the question

  • 1 points: Answers the question but has less than 25 words

  • 2 points: Answers the question and has 25 words or more

Your thoughts about the remixing process

  • 0 points: Does not provide the required information or does not answer the question

  • 1 point: Answers the question but has less than 25 words

  • 2 points: Answers the question and has 25 words or more
